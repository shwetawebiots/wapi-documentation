Wapi - Core Features

Core tools designed to make teamwork effortless, organized, and always in sync.

Authentication & Profile Setup

Smooth, secure onboarding for every user.

  • Email-based Authentication:
    Ensure valid and trusted user accounts.
  • OTP Verification:
    Add instant, secure identity confirmation.
  • Password Recovery:
    Reset forgotten passwords easily.
  • Quick Profile Setup:
    Set profile picture, name, and basic preferences.
  • Multi-Profile Essentials:
    Designed to support future workspace-based accounts.

Real-time Chat System

Fast and expressive messaging built for everyday conversations.

  • 1:1 Private Chat:
    Direct conversations anytime.
  • Group Chat:
    Create small or large conversation groups.
  • Rich Messaging:
    Text, emojis, mentions, media, and more.
  • Seen & Delivered Status:
    Know message progress.
  • Typing Indicators:
    Real-time typing updates.
  • Chat Search:
    Quickly find earlier messages or files.
  • Broadcast Messaging:
    Create a broadcast channel to send the same message to multiple users at once.
  • Chat Lock:
    Lock individual chats with a 4-digit or 6-digit password for added privacy.
  • Disappearing Messages:
    Enable messages to automatically disappear after a selected time.

File Sharing & Media

Share anything instantly inside conversations.

  • Multi-format Sharing:
    Share images, videos, documents, audio, and more.
  • File Gallery:
    View all shared files neatly sorted by type.
  • Media Preview:
    Check images and videos without leaving the chat.
  • File Size Limits:
    Control max file sizes per user.
  • In-Chat Camera Sharing:
    Capture photos or videos directly from the camera and share them instantly in chat.

Stories & Media Sharing

A social way to share life moments with friends and followers.

  • Create Stories:
    Share photos or short videos that auto-expire.
  • Story Viewers List:
    See who has viewed your story.
  • Privacy Controls:
    Make stories public, private, or close-friends only.
  • Quick Reactions:
    Send emojis on stories instantly.
  • Media Compression:
    Auto-optimize story uploads.
  • Story Replies:
    Reply directly to a user’s story for quick and private interactions.
  • Story Mute:
    Mute specific users’ stories to hide their updates from your feed.

Audio & Video Calling (WebRTC)

High-quality calls designed for smooth and stable communication.

  • HD Voice & Video:
    Clear call quality across devices.
  • Mute/Unmute & Camera Controls:
    Simple call controls.
  • Call Status Indicators:
    Connection and network insights.
  • Peer-to-Peer Communication:
    Low-latency WebRTC streaming.
  • Multi-User Call Support:
    Connect multiple participants.

Notifications

Smart alerts that keep users informed without overwhelming them.

  • Message Alerts:
    Get notified for chats and mentions.
  • Call Notifications:
    Incoming call popups with accept/decline.
  • Story Notifications:
    Alerts for new stories from friends.
  • Custom Notification Settings:
    Enable/disable specific alerts.
  • Read Indicators:
    Quickly know what you’ve already seen and what’s new.
