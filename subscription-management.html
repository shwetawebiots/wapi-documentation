Subscription Management

Overview

Subscription Management allows you to view and manage your current subscription plan, billing information, and upgrade options. This module provides complete control over your WAPI service subscription and billing cycle.

Subscription Control

  • Current Plan Details: View your current subscription plan and its features
  • Billing Information: Access your billing history and payment details
  • Plan Upgrades: Upgrade to higher-tier plans with additional features
  • Renewal Management: Manage automatic renewal settings and billing cycles
  • Usage Tracking: Monitor your usage against plan limits

Plan Management

  • Plan Comparison: Compare features across different subscription tiers
  • Downgrade Options: Switch to lower-tier plans when needed
  • Payment Method: Update payment information and billing preferences
  • Invoice Access: Download and review billing invoices
  • Subscription Status: Check subscription status and validity period
