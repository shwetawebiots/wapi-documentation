Welcome to Wapi!

Wapi – Social Messaging & Real-Time Communication Platform

Wapi is a social communication platform built for fast, expressive, and secure conversations. Powered by React, Node.js, Socket.io, and WebRTC, it brings real-time chat, social features, media sharing, and smooth audio/video calls into one simple experience. Whether you're messaging friends, sharing moments, or chatting in groups, Wapi offers a clean and responsive interface that works effortlessly across all devices.

Real-Time Messaging

Instant chat with text, media, voice notes, and smooth message delivery.

Voice & Video Calling

High-quality audio/video calls with WebRTC and essential call controls.

Social & Status Features

Friend requests, profiles, online presence, and temporary status stories.

Group Conversations

Create and manage group chats with an organized multi-user interface.

Media & File Sharing

Fast sharing of images, videos, documents, audio, and more.

Personalization & Security

Custom themes, wallpapers, user settings, OTP verification, and secure sessions.
