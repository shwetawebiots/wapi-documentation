Wapi - Advanced Features

Smart tools built for expressive communication, privacy, and social interaction.

Audio & Video Calling Suite

Stay connected with high-quality real-time communication.

  • WebRTC Integration:
    Make crystal-clear in-browser video and audio calls.
  • Screen Sharing:
    Share your screen or application window during calls.
  • Group Calls:
    Add multiple participants with stable connections.
  • Call Controls:
    Toggle camera, mute, switch devices.
  • Ring & Call Status:
    Incoming alerts, busy indicators, and call states.
  • Network Handling:
    Automatic quality adjustments for low bandwidth.

Social Profile & User Identity

Create meaningful connections with rich personal profiles.

  • Profile Customization:
    Name, bio, avatar, and status.
  • Username Search:
    Find users effortlessly.
  • Last Seen & Online Status:
    Real-time activity indicators.
  • Social Links:
    Add personal links or handles.
  • Profile Privacy:
    Control who sees what.
  • Contact Sync:
    Easily find friends across devices.
  • Verified User Badge:
    Blue tick to identify verified users via plan purchase or admin approval.

Advanced Messaging Tools

A powerful chat experience built for fast and expressive communication.

  • Message Reactions:
    Emoji responses on messages.
  • Mentions & Tagging:
    Highlight users in groups.
  • Voice Notes:
    Record and send audio clips instantly.
  • Forwarding & Replying:
    Keep conversations organized.
  • Chat Filters:
    Sort by media, files, links, or unread.
  • Dynamic Chat Loading:
    Seamless infinite scroll.
  • Multiple Message Selection:
    Perform bulk actions like delete, forward, favourite and download in one go.
  • Location Sharing:
    Share your live or current location directly in chat with other users.
  • Pinned Messages:
    Pin up to three important messages in a chat and set how long they remain pinned.

Stories & Moments

Share your daily life through quick, engaging updates.

  • Photo & Video Stories:
    Temporary posts that disappear automatically.
  • Viewers List:
    See who viewed your story.
  • Reaction Replies:
    One-tap emoji reactions.
  • Privacy Controls:
    Public, private, or close-friends lists.
  • Auto Compression:
    Optimizes media for quick uploads.
  • Story Highlights:
    Save important stories permanently.
  • Enable/Disable Stories:
    Turn the entire story section on or off directly from the admin panel.
  • Story Replies:
    Reply directly to a user’s story for quick and private interactions.
  • Story Mute:
    Mute specific users’ stories to hide their updates from your feed.

Group Management & Controls

Build and manage communities with flexible settings.

  • Group Creation:
    Public, private, or invite-only groups.
  • Role Assignments:
    Admin, moderator, and member roles.
  • Join Requests:
    Approve or deny new members.
  • Group Announcements:
    Pinned posts and broadcast messages.
  • Admin Tools:
    Remove, mute, or restrict members.
  • Group Media Hub:
    View all files shared inside the group.

Media & File Handling

A smooth sharing experience for all types of content.

  • Large File Uploads:
    Supports photos, videos, audio, and documents.
  • Media Preview:
    Inline previews without downloading.
  • Auto Compression:
    Optimize large videos and photos.
  • Chat File Gallery:
    See all shared media in one view.
  • File Type Controls:
    Manage supported formats.

Notifications & Activity Alerts

Stay informed without interruptions.

  • Message Alerts:
    Fast and reliable delivery.
  • Call Alerts:
    Incoming call popups.
  • Story Notifications:
    See when new stories are posted.
  • Unread Counter:
    Track messages across chats.
  • Custom Notification Options:
    Per-chat settings.
  • Sound, Badge & Popup Controls:
    Configure alert styles.

Privacy & Security Controls

Protect conversations with powerful privacy tools.

  • End-to-End Safe Structure:
    Strong encryption for messages.
  • Two-Step Verification:
    Secure login with OTP.
  • Session Management:
    Track and revoke active devices.
  • Block & Report System:
    Handle unwanted interactions.
  • Media Visibility Controls:
    Hide or restrict media access.
  • Account Protection Settings:
    Password, recovery mail, and backup codes.
  • SMS Gateway Options:
    Choose the preferred SMS provider directly from the admin panel.
  • Custom SMS Gateway Support:
    Add and use your own SMS gateway for message delivery.

Explore & Social Discovery

Connect, discover, and grow your social circle.

  • User Discovery:
    Explore trending or suggested profiles.
  • Friend Requests:
    Add or accept new connections.
  • Search Filters:
    Find people by name or interest.
  • Recommended Users:
    Smart suggestions based on interactions.
  • Trending Stories:
    See what’s popular within your network.
  • Social Feed Expansion: Future-ready design for upcoming social modules.
